FAQs

Find answers to common questions here.

What is KADRO VIP PRIVATE EDUCATION?

KADRO VIP PRIVATE EDUCATION is a private educational institution offering academic support for middle school and high school students, along with specialized YKS exam preparation.

Who are your teachers?

Our educators are highly experienced professionals dedicated to helping students succeed through personalized teaching methods.

What subjects do you offer tutoring in?

We offer tutoring in mathematics, science, language arts, history, foreign languages, and more, including test preparation.

Do you provide one-on-one tutoring?

Yes, we offer both one-on-one tutoring and small group classes to meet different learning needs.

What is the YKS exam?

The YKS exam is a standardized test required for university admissions in Turkey. It consists of the TYT, AYT, and YDT sections.

Who can enroll in your YKS preparation program?

Students who have previously taken the YKS and want to improve their scores, as well as first-time test takers, can join our program.

How can I enroll in a program?

You can enroll by visiting our office or calling us.

What are the tuition fees?

Tuition fees vary depending on the program. Contact us for detailed pricing information.

How can I contact KADRO VIP PRIVATE EDUCATION?

You can reach us via phone, email, or visit our office during business hours. We're happy to assist you!
